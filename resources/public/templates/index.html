What do we do?

Combat Paintball is a fantastic day out for all occasions especially for birthday parties, stag/hen do's and team building days. At Combat Paintball we specialise in catering for all groups of different types and ages whether it be kids, adrenaline junkies or Rambo imitators.

Where are we?

We are situated in Lathom, 5 minutes from Skelmersdale and Ormskirk. Just a short distance from M58 which is easily accessible from Liverpool, Manchester, St Helens, Warrington, Bolton and Blackpool. Postcode: WN8 8UT. See below for exact details and how to find us.

How do I sign up?

To book a game or for more information simply call 01695 555137. We will be able to answer any queries over the phone and can book you in straight away. We have some great deals available for all group sizes all year round.

Fun and excitement. No matter what you enjoy!

Our games are all designed to allow teams to work together and communicate whilst completing the objective of the game. Games can be played as fast or slow paced as you make it, whether you love to run around senseless or camp off like a sniper all is possible at Combat Paintball. We have many games involving flags, balls, bells and dummies all surrounded by woodland combined with trucks, towers and forts.

Great location. Accessible throughout the north west.

The site is located just off Firswood road, Lathom, WN8 8UT. Easily accessible throughout the north west including Liverpool, Manchester, Blackpool and Chester

Splat master. What about the kids?

SplatMasters are a low impact paintball gun making it ideal for younger children and those who are uncertain about traditional paintball. Splat Master was engineered and is especially for kids aged 8+. Those that are new to the SplatMaster experience will start by competing on a target range. Next is the target course to see Who can complete and shoot the most targets in the fastest time. Finally off we go to the village for a real paintball game. A fantastic introduction to paintball that the kids will talk about for weeks. The Splat Master Package has a special flat rate of £120.00 per group and MUST be paid in full at least one week in advance to reserve a game. There is no penalty for falling short of the 8 players since this is a flat rate package.
